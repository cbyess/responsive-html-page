    Pirate Ship Protocol

    Today we'll discuss proper pirate ship protocol. What is it? I've no idea. I've never been on a pirate ship. Cool pirates went extinct, what, like 500 years ago? Way too long, man. That said, so general rules are still to be expected, such as:

    • Don't puke where you sleep.
    • Don't stare when someone has to lean off the edge of the boat to do their business.
    • Don't shout 'land ho' as a joke. It's deceiving.

    Beyond the above, it's best to take a bath at least once a month, just for principle if nothing else.

    Duel Etiquette

    Ahh duels, the old way of settling scores or just stabbing someone that beat you in a card game. The rules are thus:

    • Peg legs are legal.
    • You must be drunk to duel.
    • A cheering crowd of pirates must surround you.

    Attempt to settle your problems by drawing first blood, not killing one another. If you fail, changes are there won't be enough pirates to run the ship.
